About

THE TALES OF THE SEVEN LAKES OF SAN PABLO CITY is a creative project created by undergraduate students pursuing their Bachelor of Multimedia Arts at NU Laguna. Our project is a collaboration with the San Pablo City Tourism Office, designed to promote cultural appreciation through the enchanting world of folktales, as shared by the locals of San Pablo City.

We are proud to be working hand-in-hand with the San Pablo City Tourism Office, an organization dedicated to preserving and promoting the city's cultural and historical assets. This partnership not only enhances the authenticity of our project but also reinforces the importance of collaborative efforts in preserving and celebrating local heritage.

At the heart of our project lies the seamless integration of multimedia arts. Drawing from the disciplines of illustration, animation, graphic design, and storytelling, our team of passionate students aims to bring these folktales to life in a visually captivating and immersive manner. Through this artistic lens, we seek to ignite a renewed interest in the traditional tales that have been treasured by the community for years.
